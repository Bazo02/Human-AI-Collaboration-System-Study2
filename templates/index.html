Decision-Making Study

In this study, you will evaluate a series of loan applications. For each application, your task is to decide whether the loan should be Approved or Rejected.

The study has two parts: you will complete 12 decisions without AI support and 12 decisions with AI support. The order of the two parts may vary between participants.

Before the first case, you will be shown a brief Guidelines page describing how to interpret the case information and, in the second part of the study, how to interpret the AI recommendation and explanation.

By continuing, you confirm that you understand the task and agree to participate in this study.
